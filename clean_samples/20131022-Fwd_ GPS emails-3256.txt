Apply to be a GPS Mentee!

GPS seeks to provide students with confidential one-on-one dialogue regarding issues of sexual orientation and/or gender identity.  Peer mentors are available to discuss a wide range of issues relevant to the Northwestern community, including LGBTQ-identified students and their allies. If you are interested in having a mentor, please complete the attached form and email it to northwesterngps@gmail.com.

If you'd like to learn more about the program, check out the "GPS" section on the Rainbow Alliance website.
