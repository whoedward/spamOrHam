> New to Sporcle? Not sure if you're ready to sign up? Check out these FAQs for more information!
>
>  
> What is Sporcle?
> Sporcle is a trivia quiz website in which users have a set time limit to name all the items within a given subject, such as presidents of the USA or countries in Europe. (Definition courtesy of Wikipedia.) You can check out their website here! http://www.sporcle.com/
>
> How many people are allowed on a team?
> Maximum 3 members per team. However, you may also sign up solo, as a team of two, or as one person willing to join a team. Fellows are also encouraged to participate (limit one fellow per team).
>
> How many teams are allowed per res college?
> As many as you like! There is no limit to how many teams may sign up per res college.
>
> What are the Sporcle quiz topics?
> The quiz topics include everything ranging from countries of the world to math to Disney.
>
> How does the tournament work?
> This is a double-elimination bracket tournament. One quiz will be played per round.
>
> When/where is the tournament?
> The tournament will take place Saturday, April 25th from 1:00-3:30 PM in Harris Hall 107.
>
> Are there prizes?
> Yes! First prize is one $50 Amazon gift card per team member. Second prize is one $25 ThinkGeek gift card per team member.
>
> Is there food?
> Free Jimmy John's will be served.
>
> How do I join in on the fun? Are there costs?
> Joining is easy! Just sign up here at http://goo.gl/forms/21w0jr4uWm before Friday, April 24th at midnight. It's completely free! Feel free to email christiejeung2017@u.northwestern.edu if you have further questions.
