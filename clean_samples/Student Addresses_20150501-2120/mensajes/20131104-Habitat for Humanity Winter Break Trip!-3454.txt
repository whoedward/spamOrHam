HABITAT FOR HUMANITY WINTER BREAK TRIPS!

This winter break, Northwestern University Habitat for Humanity is sending not one but TWO trips to the Gulf Coast! We are sending one trip to NEW ORLEANS, LA, and another to MOBILE, AL. 

ANYONE can go on this trip--no Habitat experience is necessary! Get pumped for opportunities to have fun on the coastal beaches while building homes in the warm South! 

Still not convinced to go? See what others have to say about break trips and Habitat in general here: http://youtu.be/UrPpYreR74U (credit: Emily Hittner)

Trip information:
Dates: December 14-23, 2013
Locations: New Orleans, LA and Mobile, AL
Cost: $300 (New Orleans) and $275 (Mobile). This covers ALL transportation, accommodations, and most meals.
