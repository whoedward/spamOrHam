Field Day competition begins on Monday in RCB Penny Wars!

Residential College Board and your philanthropy chairs will be collecting money on Monday and Tuesday.
The Rock - Monday from 10am-3pm
Lisa's Cafe - Tuesday from 10am-3pm

Drop coins in your RC's container to add to your score and drop bills in your competitors' containers to take away from their scores. The RCs with the highest score in each size category will win field day trophies, points for field day, and donations to their Relay for Life teams.

Large: Slivka, CCI, Shepard, and Willard
Medium: PARC, Jones, ISRC, and CRC
Small: CCS, Chapin, and Hobart

All proceeds will be donated to the American Cancer Society through Relay for Life
