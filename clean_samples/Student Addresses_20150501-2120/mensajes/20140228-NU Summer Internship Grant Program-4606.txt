The Summer Internship Grant Program exists to empower Northwestern undergraduate students to gain professional experience without financial frustration. As a SIGP Fellow this is achieved through receiving $2500.00 towards an unpaid summer internship and participating in a variety of University Career Services designed career development programs and projects.

For more information, go to this link:
http://www.northwestern.edu/sigp/index.html
