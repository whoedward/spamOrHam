You are invited to a faculty dinner with 

Daniel Cantor

Professor in the School of Communications

THIS FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 30th at 6:00 PM

in Hinman Dining Hall

Dan Cantor is an acting teacher in SoC. He has taught numerous acting sequences throughout his time here at NU and was awarded the 2010 SoC Clarence Simon Award for Outstanding Teaching and Mentoring. Professor Cantor has performed in numerous theaters across the country and has held roles on Law and Order, Law and Order: Special Victims Unit, Law and Order: CI,Conviction, The Sopranos, As The World Turns, and the South Korean series, Asphalt Man. 
Come have dinner with him and other J1F residents; ask him about his acting jobs, his teaching jobs, or anything else you'd like -- it's an informal dinner!
