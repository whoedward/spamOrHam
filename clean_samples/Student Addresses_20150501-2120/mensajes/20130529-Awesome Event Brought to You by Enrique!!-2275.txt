Hey everyone,

Check out this event that the National Association of Hispanic Journalists is producing!!!


"Angelica Atondo, a three-time Emmy nominated Latina who co-anchors Noticias Univision Chicago, the highest rated Spanish-language newscast in Chicago, will be coming to speak this Thursday, May 30th at 11 a.m. in Fisk 311 for the Northwestern chapter of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists. Some of her work has also appeared on CNN in Espanol. She will talk about her experiences with journalism in multiple countries and also about the importance of Spanish-speaking viewers in the United States. 

Please follow this link to RSVP for the event: tinyurl.com/atondo 

If you RSVP by Wednesday morning you will be able to receive her book "El Secreto Para Lograrlo: Caminos con Corazon (The Secret of Achievement: Heartways)" in which she details her successful path in the world of Spanish media and inspires the youth to pursue their passions."

If you would like more information here is a link to the Facebook event: https://www.facebook.com/events/565528313467253/
