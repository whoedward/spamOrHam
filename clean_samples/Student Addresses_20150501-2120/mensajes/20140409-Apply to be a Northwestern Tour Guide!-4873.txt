Do you love NU? Do you want to share your excitement for the university and your experiences here with prospective students and families?

Being a Northwestern University Tour Guide is an incredible opportunity to provide prospies with a student perspective of life at Northwestern and join a great community of other tour guides on campus -- all while getting paid to do it! We are looking for students with a passion for the university and a desire to share about their diverse experiences on campus. Students of any year can apply!

How to apply:

STEP 1: Fill out the Google form at the following link: http://goo.gl/n4o7by

STEP 2: Download the application at the following link:http://goo.gl/mAs6Qv
Complete the application, and email it to NUTourGuideApplication@gmail.com with your name in the subject line. Also attach a recent photo of yourself to this email so that we can recognize those who attend interviews.

You must complete both of the above steps by 5pm on Friday, April 11th in order to be considered for an interview. Not all applicants will receive an interview. For those who do receive interviews, they will take place May 5-9th. You will be notified about interview selection by the last week of April.

If you have any questions, please contact NUTourGuideApplication@gmail.com.
