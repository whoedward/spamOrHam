    Editorial Board Application, Vol. 7 (2013-2014)

    Due Date: Sunday, October 27, 11:59 p.m. CST

    The Northwestern Interdisciplinary Law Review (ISSN: 1945-2233) is now accepting applications for its 2013-2014 editorial board. The NILR is an undergraduate-run journal that publishes interdisciplinary legal scholarship written by law professors, students, and practitioners.

    Strong candidates will be able to clearly articulate their interest in the interdisciplinary study of law as well as demonstrate exceptional analytical and writing abilities. Recent alumni include finalists and winners of prestigious fellowships, such as the Rhodes and the Fulbright. Alumni have also been accepted to Harvard, Yale, NYU, and Georgetown law schools, among others.

    Please find the application attached. You can submit questions and completed applications via e-mail to nilr@u.northwestern.edu. For more information and to look at our most recent volume, please visit http://www.nilr.org.
