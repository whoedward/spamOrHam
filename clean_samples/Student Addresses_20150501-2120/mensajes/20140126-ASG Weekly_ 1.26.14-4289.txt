Looking for a place to live next year? Attend the Winter Housing Fair to get it taken care of now.

To learn more, check out the Facebook event here. 

Study Abroad Info Session for Bienen Students

Bienen students- Interested in studying abroad? Confused with the application process? Come to MAB 114 at 4:30 on Wednesday January 29th and speak to representatives from the Study Abroad office and IES, as well as Dr. Garton. Each speaker will give a brief presentation on the process followed by an open Q&A and an opportunity to approach the representatives with individual questions. 

To learn more, check out the Facebook event here.
