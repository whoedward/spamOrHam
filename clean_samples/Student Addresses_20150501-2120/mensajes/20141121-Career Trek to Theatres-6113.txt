The Office of Residential Academic Initiatives and Northwestern Career Advancement have set up a "career trek" for intrepid res college members to meet alumni who work in theatre. The trek will take place on Friday, December 5. Stop one is Adventure Stage Chicago (Winifred Ward and Orlin Corey Award winning theatre producing works for young audiences). Stop two is Lookingglass Theatre (Tony Award winning theatre primarily focusing on works for adults). You can have backstage access to alumni and learn about the diversity of career paths within these nationally recognized theatre companies.

 

Submit this form by MONDAY, NOVEMBER 24 to reserve a spot: http://goo.gl/forms/Sh4uHexH7G. The trek is FREE and space is limited.

 

Trek itinerary:

9am- board bus at 1835 Hinman

10-11:30am- Adventure Stage

11:30am- board bus

11:30am-12pm- boxed lunches to eat between stops

12-1:30pm- Lookingglass

1:30pm- board bus
