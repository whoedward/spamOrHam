Hello all!

The Weinberg Student Advisory Board is pleased to begin accepting nominations for the 2014 Weinberg Teaching Awards. This is an opportunity to recognize faculty members and graduate students for their excellence in teaching. The goal of these awards is to generate positive recognition for individuals who bring out the true greatness of this institution: superior academic instruction. Awards are offered in the following categories:

Weinberg College Distinguished Teaching Award, which is given to three members of the college faculty ($5000 stipend)

The Weinberg College Outstanding Graduate Student Teacher Award, which is given to three teaching assistants, usually each from a different area of WCAS ($1000 stipend)

Weinberg College Award for Excellence in Mentoring Undergraduate Research, which is given to a faculty member who has excelled in supervising lab work, independent study courses, honors work, summer research, etc. ($5000 stipend)

Weinberg College Community Building Award recognizes faculty who foster a sense of community both inside and outside the classroom and who make students feel valued as members of the Northwestern intellectual community ($5000 stipend)
Weinberg College Award for Outstanding Freshman Advising is given to three members of the College faculty that have made a major impact in their role as a Freshman advisor ($1,500 stipend)

In order to nominate a professor or student teacher for this honor, please fill out the online nomination form by Monday, March 10 at http://www.weinberg.northwestern.edu/faculty/teaching-curriculum/teaching-awards/student-information/nomination.html

For more information on the nomination process, please visit this page: http://www.weinberg.northwestern.edu/faculty/teaching-curriculum/teaching-awards/student-information/index.html

If you have any questions about the nomination process, please feel free to contact one of the Student Advisory Vice Presidents, Lindsay Jagla (LindsayJagla2015@u.northwestern.edu) or Laura Stoughton (LauraStoughton2015@u.northwestern.edu).

We look forward to receiving your nominations!

Thank you,

The Weinberg Student Advisory Board
