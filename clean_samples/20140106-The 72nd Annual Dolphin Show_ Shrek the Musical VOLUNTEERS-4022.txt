Looking for a way to get involved with America’s Largest Student Produced Musical?

 

The 72nd Annual Dolphin Show: SHREK THE MUSICAL is looking for talented and driven run crew members to assist backstage with:

Set/Props

Makeup

Lighting

Costumes

AND ~*Dragon Puppeteers*~

 

Crew view will be January 17, with training January 18. All run crew members will be expected through tech week (Jan. 19 – Jan 23), brush up (Jan. 30) and at all performances (Jan. 24, 25, 26, 31, and Feb. 1)

 

Don’t miss out on this great opportunity to get involved, learn a lot, hang out with the incredible cast and crew, join our Dolphin family, and bring this beautiful fairytale to life.

 

If you are interested please email Production Managers Jack Eidson and Izzy Garcia at pm@nudolphinshow.org
