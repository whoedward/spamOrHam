Supplies for Dreams Northwestern Chapter - First General Member Meeting!

	The Northwestern Chapter of Supplies for Dreams is having its first general member meeting on Tuesday at 8:15pm in Annenberg 101, and we want YOU to come! 

The chapter's main focus will be increasing the presence of Supplies for Dreams to campus, as well as providing its general members with opportunities to take part in volunteer days, education panels, and lots of other exciting events throughout the year. 

If you are curious about what exactly Supplies for Dreams is, please visit our website at http://www.suppliesfordreams.org/.
